Underground river, Puerto princesa

The Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park or the Puerto Princesa Underground River is located about 80 kilometers north of Puerto Princesa in Palawan. The Puerto Princessa Underground River runs through caves beneath the St. Paul Mountain Range. It is bounded to the north by St. Paul Bay and to the east by the Babuyan River. The Puerto Princesa Underground River is the longest cave in the Philippines, with a total of 8.2 kilometers in length.

Location: Puerto Princesa, Palawan
