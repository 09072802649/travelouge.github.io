Langungobingob Cave

The main chamber of Calbiga Caves is Langun, followed by Upper Gobingob which has also been fully explored. That is why another name for this cave system is Langun-Gobingob Caves. This cave system is home to several bat species, cave crickets, cave spiders, centipedes, snakes, and red and white crabs. Inside, you will be taken to an otherworldly landscape with enormous chambers that resemble coliseums and are built on top of subterranean lakes, rivers, and natural springs. It is highly recommended to wear gum boots to have some sort of protection against the ankle-deep bat guano crawling with beetles and muddy areas of the cave.

Location: Eastern Samar
