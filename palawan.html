Hidden beach, El nido, Palawan

Hidden Beach in El Nido, Palawan, is best known for its white sand, azure waters, and towering karst formations that conceal it, thus the name.From your boat, you can swim or ride a kayak to get to the beach. Hidden Beach is one of the best attractions on the list of El Nido island-hopping tours that you have to visit during your trip to the Philippines.

Location: El nido, Palawan
