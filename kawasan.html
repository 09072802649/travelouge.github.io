Kawasan Falls

Kawasan Falls in Cebu takes the top spot on this list because everything about this attraction is picturesque. This waterfall has two tiers surrounded by limestone canyon walls with a lush jungle at the back. The first tier is the main waterfall and features a height of about 40 meters. At the bottom of this tier lies a deep swimming hole. The second tier is about 20 meters high and is often less crowded. You will need to trek for 10 minutes to reach the second tier from the first. Kawasan is also famous because of its clear turquoise water at the base of the waterfall tiers. This color is incredible to look at and the reason why locals call Kawasan the “Gatorade Factory.”

Location: Algria, Cebu
