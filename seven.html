Seven Falls

Seven Falls is a collection of waterfalls located in the highlands of South Cotabato. The existence of seven waterfalls in one area is what makes Seven Falls a unique water attraction. All seven waterfalls receive water from Lake Sebu, the region’s most prominent watershed. General Santos City is the closest major city to Lake Sebu town and Seven Falls. The city’s Bulaong Terminal has vans that regularly go to Lake Sebu. When you reach Lake Sebu, you can hire a motorcycle to take you to the first two waterfalls in Barangay Seloton.

Location: South Cotabato
