Intramuros, Manila

Known as the historic center of Manila and the political, educational, and religious hub of the Spanish Empire in the Philippines, Intramuros was once an opulent walled city strategically positioned along Manila Bay. From the late 16th to the early 19th centuries, it served as the center of Spanish power. The walls, which encircled what was then the entire city of Manila, were built between 1590 and 1872..

Location: Intramuros, Manila
